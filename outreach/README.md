May 2026 competitive-landscape research. Each is a starting point — read
the target's recent public work before sending, and personalise the
opening line to something specific they shipped or posted in the last
two weeks.

## Sequencing

Send no more than two of these per week. Each one is a relationship that
needs follow-up. Don't blast them out and let them rot.

Suggested order: 1 → 4 → 3 → 2 → 5. Reppel and Mastra are the highest
expected value; Mert is the cheapest because Solana retweet weight
cascades; Shaw waits until the ElizaOS plugin is in PR; Skyfire is a
relationship play that doesn't need to be first.

## Discipline

- Never send the templates verbatim. Personalise the opener.
- Drop the receipt link (`vdmnexus.com/r/c9710ea7-9e1f-46ee-aaa9-903a536ae12e`)
  in every message. The receipt is the trojan horse.
- One specific ask per message. Not "advice" — a specific question or
  a specific PR review or a specific intro.
- After a reply: two-minute follow-up summarising next steps, closes the
  loop and gives them a reason to stay engaged.
- Track outcomes in `STATUS.md` if it turns into work, or in your own
  CRM if you have one.
